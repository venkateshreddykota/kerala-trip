Kerala Trip Plan – 14 to 19 August 2025

From Ongole to Ernakulam – Sunrise, Chocolate, and Backwaters!

📅 Day 0 – 14 Aug (Thu): Departure from Ongole

🚆 Board train from Ongole at 5:30 PM to Ernakulam Junction.

🛏️ Overnight train journey.

📅 Day 1 – 15 Aug (Fri): Arrival & Travel to Munnar

  • 🌄 Arrive at Ernakulam around 9:00–10:00 AM
  • 🚗 Travel to Munnar (~4.5 hours)
  • 🍫 Visit Homemade Chocolate Factory
  • 🛍️ Explore local tea, spices, and Munnar town
  • 🛏️ Stay in Munnar

📅 Day 2 – 16 Aug (Sat): Sunrise & Munnar Sightseeing

  • 🌅 Early morning trip to Top Station for sunrise
  • 🌿 Visit:
    • Mattupetty Dam
    • Echo Point
    • Tea Museum
    • Photo Point
  • 🛏️ Stay in Munnar

📅 Day 3 – 17 Aug (Sun): Munnar → Alleppey

  • 🚗 Travel to Alleppey (~5.5 hrs)
  • 🚤 Check-in to Houseboat by 12:00 PM
  • 🍛 Enjoy meals and scenic cruise on backwaters
  • 🛏️ Overnight on houseboat or Alleppey hotel

📅 Day 4 – 18 Aug (Mon): Alleppey → Kochi

  • 🚗 Travel to Kochi (~1.5 hrs)
  • 🏝️ Visit:
    • Fort Kochi
    • Chinese Fishing Nets
    • Marine Drive
    • Jew Street & Palace
  • 🛏️ Stay near Ernakulam station

📅 Day 5 – 19 Aug (Tue): Return to Ongole

🚆 Board return train from Ernakulam Junction to Ongole

❤️ Enjoy your Kerala journey filled with memories, chocolates & sunrise magic!
